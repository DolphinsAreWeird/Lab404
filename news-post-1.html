Showcasing My Happy Thai Neighbourhood at CMKL

January 17, 2025

We were thrilled to present My Happy Thai Neighbourhood regularly at CMKL's Local Project Showcases! This interactive game, designed to educate players on recognizing and combating cybercrimes, was met with enthusiastic feedback from attendees.

The showcase offered an excellent opportunity to share our vision of blending education and entertainment through AI-driven solutions tailored for Southeast Asia. Engaging with local students, educators, and innovators provided valuable insights to further enhance the game.

We're excited to continue refining My Happy Thai Neighbourhood and exploring more opportunities to make a positive impact in the community. Stay tuned for more updates!
