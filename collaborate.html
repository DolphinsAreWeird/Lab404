Research Interns

We are looking for passionate students interested in AI, educational technology, digital immersion, and critical thinking tools. These internships offer hands-on experience with cutting-edge research projects.

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled student (any university)
  • Strong interest in LAB404 research areas
  • Relevant technical skills (vary by project)
  • Good communication and teamwork abilities

Duration: Varies (typically project-based or semester-long)

Application: Contact us for current openings and specific project needs.
