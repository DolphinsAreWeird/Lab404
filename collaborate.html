Research Interns

We are looking for passionate students interested in AI, educational technology, digital immersion, and critical thinking tools. These internships offer hands-on experience with cutting-edge research projects.

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate program
  • Strong interest in at least one of our research areas
  • Basic programming skills (Python, JavaScript, or similar)
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities

Duration: 3-6 months, with possibility of extension

Application Deadline: Ongoing

We welcome professionals and academics who wish to collaborate on specific projects or research initiatives. These collaborations can be structured as visiting researchers, consultants, or project partners.

Areas of Interest

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Educational Technology and Gamification
  • Digital Art and Cultural Preservation
  • Critical Thinking and Cognitive Enhancement Tools

If you have expertise in these or related areas and are interested in collaborating, we would love to hear from you.
