January 17, 2025

AI Engineering and Innovation Summit 2024 Highlights

On December 13th 2024, Professor Lorenzo and the team had the privilege of presenting at the AI Engineering and Innovation Summit 2024 in Bangkok, Thailand. This prestigious event brought together innovators, researchers, and technology enthusiasts to explore groundbreaking advancements in AI and their real-world applications.

Our team showcased two exciting projects: My Happy Thai Neighbourhood and TH-Hai Immersion. My Happy Thai Neighbourhood is an interactive game designed to educate players about cybersecurity through engaging gameplay, empowering them to recognize and combat cyber threats in a fun, relatable way. Meanwhile, TH-Hai Immersion leverages AI to deliver an unparalleled language and cultural learning experience.

January 17, 2025

Showcasing My Happy Thai Neighbourhood at CMKL

We were thrilled to present My Happy Thai Neighbourhood regularly at CMKL's Local Project Showcases! This interactive game, designed to educate players on recognizing and combating cybercrimes, was met with enthusiastic feedback from attendees.

The showcase offered an excellent opportunity to share our vision of blending education and entertainment through AI-driven solutions tailored for Southeast Asia. Engaging with local students, educators, and innovators provided valuable insights to further enhance the game.

We're excited to continue refining My Happy Thai Neighbourhood and exploring more opportunities to make a positive impact in the community.
