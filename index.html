Focus

The core purpose of LAB404 is to offer a framework for students to collaborate, express creativity, gain hands-on experience, and deliver meaningful projects mixing technology, interaction, software, engineering, AI, art, and design, with a practical application in mind.

Philosophy

LAB404 is an evolving learning environment empowering students to explore, experiment, innovate, and embrace failure as a path to success. Diversity fosters collaboration, mentorship, and ownership, honing essential skills for innovators and leaders.
