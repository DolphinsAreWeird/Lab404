Evolution of Innovation: Mirror AI at AI Innovation Summit 2025

January 30, 2025

2025 has kicked off with immense momentum for LAB404. Our team recently participated in the AI Innovation Summit 2025 in Bangkok, co-hosted by CMKL University and AIEI Thailand. This year, the focus was on "Versatile Intelligence," exploring how AI can adapt to complex human environments—a mission that lies at the heart of our latest developments.

The highlight of our showcase was the debut of Mirror AI. Formerly known as TH-Hai Immersion, Mirror AI has evolved far beyond its original scope. While it retains its core strength in cultural and language immersion, it has been re-engineered to be a more versatile tool for real-time interaction and personalized learning. The rebranding reflects our vision of AI as a "mirror" that reflects and enhances human potential rather than just replacing tasks.

During the summit, we demonstrated Mirror AI’s updated capability to process nuanced emotional cues and its improved architecture, making it faster and more up-to-date with current linguistic trends in Thailand. The feedback from the AIEI Thailand community was invaluable, highlighting a high demand for tools that bridge the gap between technical AI and cultural relevance.

The team demonstrating Mirror AI's live interaction capabilities.

Beyond Mirror AI, LAB404 presented updates on our broader project portfolio. We’ve spent the last few months making our systems more modular and versatile, ensuring that the technology we build today can be easily integrated into various educational and social impact sectors across Southeast Asia.

We are incredibly proud of the team’s hard work in keeping our projects at the cutting edge. Stay tuned as we prepare for more rollouts and pilot programs throughout the rest of the year!
