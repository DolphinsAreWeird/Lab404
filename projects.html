Research Focus Areas

Our multidisciplinary approach to innovation

Educational Technology

Artificial Intelligence

SmartSort

A smart waste classification and guidance system using machine learning and computer vision. Designed for public areas like tourist spots, it uses real-time camera input, visual indicators, and gentle audio cues to help users sort their waste correctly.

Planned Features:

  • Computer Vision-Based Trash Scanning
  • ML Classification & Trash Type Detection
  • LED Cues: Green for Correct Bin, Yellow for General Waste
  • Audio Feedback for Success/Error Actions
  • Real-Time Sensor Feedback for Correct Disposal
