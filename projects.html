Current Projects

Pushing the boundaries of innovation and research

MY HAPPY THAI NEIGHBORHOOD

An interactive webtoon-based game educating players in Southeast Asia about cybercrime and equipping them with practical strategies to combat online scams effectively.

Key Features & Goals:

  • Webtoon-Based Learning Platform
  • Gamified Cybersecurity & Scam Awareness
  • Targeted for Southeast Asian Audiences
  • EdTech and AI Integration
  • Goal: Create engaging, effective cybersecurity education.

TH.AI.IMMERSION

An AI-generated digital art experience transforming real-world environments into interactive, artistic impressionist renditions inspired by Thai culture.

Key Features & Goals:

  • Real-time AI Art Generation & Interaction
  • Immersive Digital Experience
  • Showcase of Thai Culture via AI Art
  • Advanced Diffusion Model Integration
  • Goal: Blend art, technology, and culture for education/aesthetics.
Contact: lorenzo@cmkl.ac.th | tthiwc@cmkl.ac.th

FALLACY HUNTER

A browser-based extension focused on text-based logical fallacy detection using pattern recognition and machine learning frameworks to enhance critical reading skills.

Key Features & Goals:

  • Advanced NLP for Fallacy Detection
  • Convenient Browser Extension
  • Real-time Feedback & Learning Aid
  • Enhancing Critical Thinking & Communication
  • Goal: Promote critical analysis of online text.
Contact: lorenzo@cmkl.ac.th | tthiwc@cmkl.ac.th

Research Focus Areas

Our multidisciplinary approach to innovation
