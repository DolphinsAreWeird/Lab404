MY HAPPY THAI NEIGHBORHOOD

An engaging interactive webtoon-based game designed to educate players in Southeast Asia about cybercrime and equip them with practical strategies to combat scams effectively.

Revolutionizing Gamified Education

  • Webtoon-Based Learning Platform
  • Gamifying Cybersecurity and Scam Awareness Education
  • Cutting-Edge Technology in EdTech and Artificial Intelligence

Project Goals

To create an engaging educational platform that effectively teaches cybersecurity awareness through gamified storytelling, making complex security concepts accessible to a broad audience in Southeast Asia.

Technology Stack

Interactive web application built with React, using AI-driven narrative generation and personalized learning pathways.

TH.AI.IMMERSION

An AI-generated digital art immersion that transforms real-world environments into interactive artistic impressionist renditions.

Redefining Digital Immersion

  • Constant Generation and Interaction
  • Experience Thailand and its beauty immersively through digital media
  • Cutting-Edge Technology Utilizing Diffusion and Generation

Project Goals

To create immersive digital experiences that showcase Thai culture and landscapes through AI-generated art, providing both educational and aesthetic value to visitors.

Technology Stack

Advanced diffusion models, computer vision, and interactive projection technologies combined with cultural data archives.

FALLACY HUNTER

A browser-based extension focused on text-based fallacy detection using pattern recognition and machine learning frameworks.

Enabling Critical Reading

  • Identify logical fallacies with NLP and Pattern detection
  • Provide feedback to help users understand and avoid fallacies
  • Enhance critical thinking and communication within papers and articles

Project Goals

To develop an accessible tool that helps users identify logical fallacies in text, promoting critical thinking and improving the quality of online discourse and academic writing.

Technology Stack

Advanced NLP models, browser extension technology, and educational content frameworks integrated with text analysis algorithms.

Project Contacts:
