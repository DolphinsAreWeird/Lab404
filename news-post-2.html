AI Engineering and Innovation Summit 2024 Highlights

January 17, 2025

On December 13th 2024, Professor Lorenzo Avi and the LAB404 team had the privilege of presenting at the AI Engineering and Innovation Summit 2024 in Bangkok, Thailand. This prestigious event brought together innovators, researchers, and technology enthusiasts to explore groundbreaking advancements in AI and their real-world applications.

Our team showcased two exciting projects: My Happy Thai Neighbourhood and TH-Hai Immersion. My Happy Thai Neighbourhood is an interactive game designed to educate players about cybersecurity through engaging gameplay, empowering them to recognize and combat cyber threats in a fun, relatable way. Meanwhile, TH-Hai Immersion leverages AI to deliver an unparalleled language and cultural learning experience, offering immersive tools that connect users to the richness of Thai traditions.

The response from the audience was incredibly encouraging. Attendees praised our efforts to blend education and entertainment with cutting-edge AI, particularly for their relevance to Southeast Asia.

The summit provided a platform for networking and exchanging ideas with industry leaders and fellow innovators, fueling our passion to refine these projects further. We are thrilled to contribute to the AI landscape in Thailand and beyond, and we look forward to advancing our vision of making technology impactful and accessible for all.
